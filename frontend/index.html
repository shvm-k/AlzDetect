▸ INTENDED USE & LIMITATIONS

AlzDetect is a research demonstration, developed and validated on a single research MRI dataset; its accuracy is reported on held-out images from that same source.

  • It is not a diagnostic tool and has not been clinically validated or regulatory-approved.
  • Results on scans from other scanners, institutions, or preprocessing pipelines may be unreliable.
  • A single prediction is not a diagnosis — Alzheimer's assessment requires clinical evaluation by qualified professionals.
