⚠ NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE Research & educational tool only. Predictions must not be used for clinical diagnosis. *78% accuracy (best of a 12-seed search; typical ≈72%) on a held-out split of a fuzzy-rebalanced training set; the 92% figure in the paper was measured on a different (now-removed) Kaggle dataset and is not reproducible with current data. This accuracy is in-distribution only — the model was trained and tested on a single MRI source, and predictions on scans from other scanners/datasets can be confidently wrong (a known domain-shift limitation). Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
